Astitva Archive Registry

The Astitva Archive provides the official catalogue of scholarly publications issued within the research programme of the Global Synergetic Foundation (GSFN). The archive brings together monographs, micro-monographs, and research studies exploring the foundations of existence, cognition, and knowledge systems.

The Three Research Series

The archive is organized into three interconnected publication series representing successive stages in a continuing philosophical and scientific inquiry.

Series Conceptual Orientation
Astitva Foundational investigations into existence, consciousness, and the philosophical structures underlying knowledge systems.
Vedic-Hṛdaya-Cakṣu Studies exploring the interpretive vision through which Vedic knowledge systems may be understood in their deeper philosophical and scientific dimensions.
Svagarbhā Advanced research on the inner generative principles of Vedic thought, including Agni, cosmology, cognition, and knowledge structures.

The conceptual trajectory of the research programme may be expressed as:

Astitva → Vedic-Hṛdaya-Cakṣu → Svagarbhā → Astitva

This cycle reflects the evolving movement of inquiry in which the investigation of existence ultimately returns to the question of being itself.
